Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • Your project description and service type

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your estimate request or inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Contact Form Processing

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. Your submission data is transmitted to Formspree's servers for delivery to us. Please review Formspree's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle data.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or analytics software. We may add basic analytics in the future, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and complete any requested services. We do not maintain a marketing database.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request deletion of any personal information you have submitted. To do so, call us at (401) 247-2040.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at (401) 247-2040 or via Instagram at @zompaplumbing.
